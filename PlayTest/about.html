Where To Find Us

The playgroup is located in the East Craigs Church Hall, which is behind the Dentist and Doctors' Surgeries in Bughtlin Market. (See Contact for full address and map).

Session Times

Sessions run Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday and Friday mornings from 9.15am to 11.15am.

Cost

Each sessions costs £5 per child, which is payable on a per term basis at the start of each term. It is very important that fees are paid on time, your child's place may be in jeopardy if fees are late.

Our Facilities

Children have a wide range of toys to play with and activities to enjoy, such as:

  • Cars, trucks, trains and planes
  • Painting (aprons provided)
  • Play dough (including rolling pins and cutters)
  • Kitchen corner with tea sets, toy cooker, microwave and food etc
  • Music corner
  • Sand table

Snack Time

Each session the children sit down in groups to enjoy a healthy and fun snack. Foods include:

  • Toast
  • Fruit
  • Crackers
  • Cheese
  • Milk or water is also provided

A small donation is requested to help provide the snack.
